\subsection{Rechnen mit ganzen Zahlen}

Regeln:\\
\begin{itemize}
    \item (+)(+) = +
    \item (-)(-) = +
    \item (-)(+) = -
    \item (+)(-) = -
\end{itemize}

\hfill \break
Example:\\
\fboxrule=0.8pt \fcolorbox{black}{lightgray}{%
    \begin{tabular}[t]{@{}l@{}}
        $(-4)+(-2)-(+5)-(-1) = ? $ \\
        $-4-2-5+1 = -10$          \\
    \end{tabular}}
